Project Architecture

A Dual-Layer System: Physical Circularity & Digital Governance

Layer A

Physical Circular Farming

12-Parameter Soil Sensor

Handheld device measuring NPK, SOM/SOC, and micronutrients (Fe, Zn) via ML estimation.

Impact: 20-32% Yield Increase, 30% Fertilizer Saving.

Arsenic Shield (3F4D+MD)

Immobilizes arsenic via Iron Oxide re-precipitation in 61 affected districts.

Impact: 9-21% Arsenic Reduction, 57% Water Saving.

Solar Cold Chain

10-ton hybrid mini cold storage with 70% reduced operating costs.

Financials: BCR > 3.0, IRR 14.47%.
Layer B

Efficiency 3.0 Blockchain Governance

Off-Chain (PostgreSQL) Source of Truth for Details (PII, History)
On-Chain (Hyperledger) Source of Truth for Integrity (Immutable Audit)

SHA-256 Integrity Link

Every transaction receives a digital signature. Any data alteration triggers a hash mismatch, preventing fraud.
