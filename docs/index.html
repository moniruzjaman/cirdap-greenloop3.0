The GreenLoop Story

How we're transforming Bangladesh's agricultural future

০১

সমস্যা: The Challenge

Bangladesh loses 4.0% of GDP annually from agricultural inefficiencies. With LDC graduation in 2026, we face a critical need for transformation.

$220 Billion Annual economic opportunity
০২

সমাধান: Our Solution

GreenLoop 3.0 introduces Efficiency-by-Design - a dual-layer system combining physical circular farming with blockchain governance.

100% Circular Bio-Ecosystem
০৩

প্রভাব: The Impact

Empowering 10 million farmers with technology, creating 50,000 youth jobs, and achieving climate resilience for 61 arsenic-affected districts.

3,500 kg CO2 offset per unit

Technology

Solution Architecture

A Tri-Layer System: Physical → Digital → Community

Layer A

Physical Circular Farming

Hardware & IoT Infrastructure

12-Parameter Soil Sensor

Handheld device measuring NPK, SOM/SOC, and micronutrients via ML estimation

20-32% Yield 30% Fertilizer

Arsenic Shield (3F4D+MD)

Immobilizes arsenic via Iron Oxide re-precipitation in 61 affected districts

9-21% Arsenic 57% Water

Solar Cold Chain

10-ton hybrid mini cold storage with 70% reduced operating costs

BCR > 3.0 IRR 14.47%
Layer B

Digital Platform & Blockchain

Governance & Transparency Layer

Hyperledger Blockchain

Immutable on-chain records for data integrity and transparency

SHA-256 Immutable

e-Bill System

Digital transaction records with farmer credit tracking

Off-Chain PostgreSQL

AI-Powered Analytics

Machine learning for crop prediction and resource optimization

Predictive Real-time
Layer C

Community & Impact

Social Transformation Layer

কৃষক কার্ড (Farmer Card)

10M+ Farmers Financial Inclusion

Youth Digital Facilitators

NEET youth trained as Agro-Service Centre operators

50,000 Jobs Skill Development

Women Empowerment

Gender-Responsive Nutritional Security
